About Our Airlines:

Iran Air is the airline carrying the flag of Iran with the official name of the Islamic Republic of Iran Airlines, which is known in Iran as Huma (Iran National Airlines) since its establishment. Iran Air, which is one of the oldest airlines operating in the Middle East, currently flies to more than 40 domestic and international destinations.

The head office of Iran Air is located in Mehr Abad Airport in Tehran and it is the main flight base of this company, where most of Iran Air's flights originate from or go to its destination for domestic flights from Mehr Abad Airport and for international flights from Imam Khomeini Airport in Tehran.
